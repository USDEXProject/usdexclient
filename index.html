Frequently asked questions

Why do we create Stable USD (USDEX)?

USDEX is the digital dollar. Like other electronic currency assets, USDEX can immediately move anywhere in the world at any time. Unlike other electronic currency assets, USDEX is stable at 1: 1 for the dollar.

It is a part in our future vision to the global economy where all assets can immediately move anywhere within 24/7.

Why is Ethereum applied in USDEX?

Ethereum is a ledger based on the decentralized blockchain, supporting smart contracts and it is the most widely used digital asset platform. USDEX follows the ERC-20 protocol which is a standard for smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. The ERC-20 standard specifies a set of standard functions for managing transactions and tracking account balances. Some advantages of ERC-20 token are as follows:

- Continuously support wallet and integrate exchanges. All suppliers who implements the ERC-20 token can support the token on their platform

- Maximum trust of end-user

- Applied in a large community, be easy to store and move assets

- Reduce liquidity risk

What is USDEX used for?

USDEX can be used to limit fluctuations in electronic currency assets, to eliminate charges of cross-border transaction and to easily move electronic currency assets or to pay assets based on blockchain.

Who can use USDEX?

USDEX allows individuals and businesses - including exchanges, wallets, payment processors, financial services - to easily use fiat money on blockchains.

Where can I use USDEX?

We want to make USDEX usable in all places where you can use the digital currency and in many places where the digital currency is not currently accepted.
